Chozen Boys

The Chozen Boys is a place for middle school boys to grow spiritually, make great friendships, and have tons of fun! Each Sunday we meet for a discussion-based Bible study, active games, downtime to build friendships, and a shared meal.

The adult workers in our group are Larry, Liam, Cole, and Alex.

Summer Camp (Blowout)

Blowout Camp is a summer camp we do every year in July. Some of the adult workers of Chozen boys may go also to act as cabin leaders. You can read more about it on Dwell's website.
